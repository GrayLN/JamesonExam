Welcome to the Gamer's Den!

In order to play our brick breaker game, you'll need either a Mobile device, keyboard, or mouse,. If playing on keyboard, you may use your left and right arrow keys to move the paddle. If playing with a mouse, just simply move your mouse around. On mobile, press where you want to go. To simply play, click on the play tab above!
